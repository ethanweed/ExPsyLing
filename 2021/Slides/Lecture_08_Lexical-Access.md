# Lexical Access


---

Word -> Meaning

---

### Aspects of lexical access

Recognition
Identification
Understanding
Naming


---

### Recognition

The word is familiar:

NIGHT is a word

NITE is not

---

### Identification

The word is distinguished from other words:

NIGHT is 
	- not MIGHT 
	- not NIGHTTIME
	- not NIGH


---

### Understanding

NIGHT *means* the time from sunset to sunrise
