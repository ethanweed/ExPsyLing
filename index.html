Experimental Psycholinguistics

Goals:

  • Become familiar with the structure of the course

Lecture and Discussion:

  • Introduction to the structure of the course
  • Questions and answers

Lab:

  • Try participating in an online experiment

Assignment:

  • Read chapters from Navarro & Foxcroft (2018)
    • Chapter 1
    • Chapter 2
  • Read chapters from Hinklelman & Kempthorne (2008)
    • Chapter 1 (Ok to skim some)
    • Chapter 2 (Ok to skim some, especially the math. I have highlighted key points)
    • Chapter 2 section 2.7 (Essential reading!)
  • Install software
    • OpenSesame (https://osdoc.cogsci.nl/3.3/download/)
    • JASP (https://jasp-stats.org/download/ )
